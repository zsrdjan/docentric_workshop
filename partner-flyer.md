# Lift Off Workshop for Partners

## Personalized Workshops to Help Your Team Succeed with Docentric AX implementation

---

## Why This Workshop?

Prepare your team to work with Docentric AX in real projects.
The **Lift Off Workshop** is a focused workshop designed for small teams that want:

- Faster onboarding
- Practical, hands-on guidance
- One ready-to-use Docentric report

**Prerequisites:** This training builds on the Docentric Learning Path.
Basic Docentric knowledge is required before joining.

---

## Workshop program

### 1. Discovery Call (1 hour)
**We start by understanding your needs:**
- Learn about your team's goals
- Select one SSRS report to transform with Docentric
- Prepare a customized plan for your workshops

### 2. Online Workshops (4 sessions × 3 hours)
**Delivered twice per week, we cover:**
- Docentric Designer essentials
- Configuration and setup in Dynamics 365 Finance
- Docentric programming and extension patterns

**Group size:** Maximum 3 attendees per company

### 3. Offline Support (4 hours total)
**Get help when you need it:**
- Four 1-hour support blocks for guidance between sessions
- Address questions as they arise in your learning journey

### 4. Wrap-Up Call (1 hour)
**Finish strong with a final review:**
- Summary of everything covered
- Q&A session
- Clear next steps for your team

### 5. Certificate of Completion
**Celebrate your achievement!**
- Each participant receives a Lift Off Program certificate or badge
